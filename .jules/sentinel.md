## 2025-05-14 - Hardening FRI Verifier against DoS and Integer Overflows
**Vulnerability:** The FRI verifier in `p3-fri` was susceptible to DoS via panics (generator sampling for excessive degrees) and OOM (arbitrary arity allocations) because it didn't validate prover-supplied reduction schedules. It also lacked checked arithmetic for height calculations and height consistency checks with input matrices.
**Learning:** In Plonky3, codeword heights in FRI are derived from trace heights plus a blowup factor. The verifier must explicitly validate that the reduction schedule provided in the proof is consistent with the expected codeword height and within the field's two-adicity and system word limits before performing any allocations or generator sampling.
**Prevention:** Always use `checked_add` and `try_fold` for sums of logarithmic dimensions. Enforce `log_arity > 0` and `<= params.max_log_arity`. Validate total height against `Val::TWO_ADICITY` and `usize::BITS`. Ensure query bit counts are safe for `challenger.sample_bits`.
